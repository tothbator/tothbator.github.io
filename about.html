Bátor Tóth invites you to a transformative event in which paintings perform instances of abstract reflections. As if entering a mirror that further expands your surroundings would be possible, you are guided, painting-by-painting, closer-and-closer to the reality of the Unknown. There you are, staring back at yourself from an alien perspective, realizing infinite new sights of understanding.
