SILVERPOINTS

Silverpoint is a drawing technique where a silver stylus is used on a specially prepared surface, creating fine, delicate lines that oxidize over time to produce a subtle, bronish-silvery-gray tone. It was widely used by Renaissance artists like Leonardo da Vinci for detailed sketches, valued for its precision and permanence.
